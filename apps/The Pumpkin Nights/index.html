Pumpkin Nights

Can you and your friends survive against the pumpkin attack!?

Someone knocked on your door and drop by letter, offering a nightshift job at a pumpkin field little do you know, it will be a horrifying decision as there something lurking in the field

Pumpkin Nights is a horror local multiplayer game where your objectives is to survive against an incoming pumpkin attack. Use your cameras, windows shutter, and conserve electricity to survive the night against the pumpkin terror! Can you and your friends survive against the pumpkin attack!? and unravel the secret mystery behind the pumpkins?

This game includes

  • 6 nights of terror to survive
  • 4 possible endings
  • custom night for custom challenge
  • scary visual
  • pumpkin monster
  • simple lore
  • local multiplayer feature
